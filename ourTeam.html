The WArriors of Magi-A

OUR TEAM THE TRUE MAGICIANS OF OUR FIRM

Bir Singh khati - Head Operations & Management


With an experience of over 20 years in hospitality and management, Mr. Bir is an exceptional employee. He started his career as a trainee and through years of hard work, now find himself as the esteemed head of operations and management at Magi-a.

Being a trainee himself at a time, he holds a great PR with the staff and has a gift of hiring and training the best staff. His experience with various fine dining restaurants like, Moti Mahal, Bercos, etc., make him a great asset to Magi-a. He also worked at the corporate manager of Yellow chillies.

And now he is working his magic in Magi-a.

Anuj - Master Chef


What is a restaurant without good food?

Chef Anuj has an experience of more than 13 years under his belt. His passion for food sets him apart. His love for food is the sole ingredient in making the amazing, magical dishes that we provide our clients with.

With an expertise in rich, world cuisines, he is adventurous and always inventing new dishes with deep research.

Sushant Kapoor - Sous Chef, Project Auditor, PR


One of our youngest employees, but do not mistake his age for his experience. Mr. Sushant has been a pioneer in the food industry from the very start. He pursued his BA(hons) in Culinary Arts from University of Huddersfield, UK (at) IHM Aurangabad with great public relations, gastronomy and the art of developing and implementing menus.

He also has a keen interest in innovating and conceptualising new ideas.

Davender Kumar - Architect & Concept Creation


If a restaurant has good food and a bad environment, it spoils the food. Mr. Davender from Magi-a has a 15-year experience into designing and concept creation.

He has designed a number of cafes, restaurants, puns and retail stores. With this experience he has an exceptional skill of bringing his client’s dream to life.

He is very particular about the needs of the client and always delivers on time.

Neha Nurse - HR & Staff Training


Ms. Neha completed her education from IHM Bombay, and has great skills in terms of staff hiring and training. She trains the staff with both, the basics of hospitality industry and then according to the client’s requirements. She ensures that the reaches the restaurant and is being trained as per the client.

Ms. Neha’s quick thinking a response time helps us to hire the right staff to suit a restaurant’s concept. .
