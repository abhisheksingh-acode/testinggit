OUR DIRECTOR

The Brain Child of Magi-A

OUR DIRECTOR

From star restaurant to cozy cafes, he has experienced it all at the young age of 18 years. It is this experience that makes him a force to be reckoned with in the business

He knows that the initial 6 months of a restaurant’s lifespan are crucial for establishing a name in the industry. He handles this time delicately along with investor, once the business is stable and the marketing is on fire, he hands over the restaurant to the investors.

With Magi-a Mr. Kuvam has placed a new benchmark in the F&B consultancy business.

Despite his experience he believes a man never stops learning, as a strives everyday to take Magi-a a step further.

Our director, Mr. Kuvam Gujral established Magi-a in 2018 when he saw that many new restaurant/ Cafe owners were struggling to run, or get services in terms of restaurant operations & stability in P&L. People get into the restaurant business expecting loads of profits with dreams of franchising out, but fail for the mere reasons of poor support in terms of staffing & services provided by the consultation firm they are linked with.

He noticed how most consultation firms would provide services to 3 to 6 months Initially and then the business tends to drop down, following which the business would suffer huge losses. If the do agree to provide support, they charge a retainer fee, which would be high and not affordable by a business owner already swimming in loss because of their previous experiences.

He started Magi-a with the goal of creating magic for these Restaurant & Cafe owners and solve all their problems in the blink of an eye. By proving the best services to our existing clients and as well as new clients, we make sure you never have to worry about business failure.

He grew up in the business, and has experienced the nuances of it first hand.
Mr Kuvam does not look at a business through the eyes of an investor. Our old Indian values of, ‘Athithi Devo Bhav’ are instilled in him. He looks at a restaurant through the eyes of its guests, which is the reason why his consultancy is on spot. His experience in the field comes from him being a restaurant owner himself of a renowned chain. His chain of fine-dining restaurants, Pan-Asia & abroad, serving Modern Indian, Chinese, Continental, Italian, Barbeque Grill Restaurants South Indian & Fusion Food are viewed as some of the best restaurants in the market. Before getting into the family business, he completed his education from Pathway World School.

He decided it was time to further expand his legacy of over 10 years and established Magi-a as his own company. With hands on experience working in the parental company, he knows the what and what nots of the business.

To Mr. Kuvam, Magi-a is the future of the F&B consultancy, and for that purpose he trains most of his Franchisees himself. Teaching them how to operate a restaurant business, while following certain SOP and also controlling the pilferages.

We are so confident in Magi-a that Mr. Kuvam states, “If you feel that our services have lacked in terms of operations and management, then you can claim your fees back.” As for him the main aim is client satisfaction. Mr. Kuvam states the worlds most expensive thing is “TRUST” Which is very important for his clients to have on him cause he is trying to create a brand and soon a legacy for them.

Mr. Kuvam is always very invested in his client’s business, as of it were his own. He finds it a privilege to be able to help his clients right from the scratch to the end of their projects, including, the designing, selection of site, a unique name for the retardant that will reflect its values. He even takes care of negotiating with landlords and trains the investors so they are well prepared to operate their outlets.

From star restaurant to cozy cafes, he has experienced it all at the young age of 12 years. It is this experience that makes him a force to be reckoned with in the business.

He knows that the initial 6 months of a restaurant’s lifespan are crucial for establishing a name in the industry. He handles this time delicately along with investor and his team.

With Magi-a Mr. Kuvam has placed a new benchmark in the F&B consultancy business.
