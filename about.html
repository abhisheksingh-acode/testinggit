About US

Let Magi-A work it’s magic on your restaurant

WHO WE ARE

Owing a restaurant/Cafe is a wholesome experience. It is much more than a business. So many memories are made in a restaurant, someone celebrating their anniversary, or a birthday, and someone else being proposed to. Over the years, restaurant have evolved from being a mere eating place, to a place people go to take a break from their busy lives and reconnect with their loved ones.

So, before you start a restaurant business, you have kept in mind all these people, give them the best, and to do that, you need the best.

Magi-a hospitality and food consultancy is a leading chain of consultancy firm, with the sole purpose of turning your restaurant into your customer’s dream restaurant. We provide consultancy and management services along with planning, designing, execution and staffing, to new and existing restaurant owners.

Magi-a provides a complete service, right from the scratch to the very end. Once you’re tied up with us, your dream is our dream

We believe in our ethics, monetary gains are secondary, customer satisfaction is always on top.

With Magi-a, you can be well assured that your business is in the right hands. Your staffing and management will be smoothly handled by us.

We have a team of professional ventilation, Interior, Kitchen-Bar Consultants including the final contractors who will execute your project. Magi-a does not charge any extra fees for third party consultations.

Our director believes in originality and creativity, which is why all our Cafés & Restaurants are uniquely themed and have different menus, designed entirely for our clients.

Our services are wide ranged and we cover everything that a restaurant business would ever need.

OUR MISSION & VISION

Magi-a was started with the mission to help restaurant owners across India and the world learn all the basics of operating a restaurant and getting the maximum profits.

Our clients are extremely important to us, and to this day, every one of them has returned with satisfied smile on their faces. We will strive to uphold that very image.

Our vision is to take Magi-a to newer heights, establish ourselves in Pan-Asia & Abroad.
